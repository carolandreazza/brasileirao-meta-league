THE PROJECT

  • Each of us has felt the emotions a soccer ball can cause. No matter how improvised or the quality of the field, the magic of the sport is part of our lives from an early age. That feeling makes each of us a passionate expert who takes every pass very seriously.
  • What if you could experience the euphoria of a football match, the excitement of a crowd and the excitement of rooting for your favorite sport from anywhere in the world, with no limits to your creativity?
  • With a simple ball pass you are transported to fantastic worlds within a map where sport and imagination will guide you. Where you will be able to collect historical pieces. Where you will connect with a community passionate about football history and who love sports, just like you.
  • The Brasileirão Binance League map inspires the future of Brazilian sport and celebrates our tradition moving towards the metaverse.
  • Join matches, customize your players, coaches and legendary figures, increase your skills with limited assets and create a world where anything is possible!
    From sporting challenges to unique and mysterious events, Meta League invites you to play in the most important league of your life.
  • Put on your boots and embark on a journey to discover every detail of the Brasileirão Binance League map in Sandbox!
